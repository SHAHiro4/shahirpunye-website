TEAMWORK AND COMUNICATION The times when a good software product that many will use could be created alone have faded away. Therefore, the ability to work in a team is perhaps one of the most important skills now. Take just two of the best developers who cannot communicate with each other, constantly argue, and cannot find common ground, and you will never finish your project. Extrapolate this problem to a larger team, and you will understand the full importance of this skill.
Creativity and Problem-Solving Don't forget not to reinvent the wheel. Good creativity is best used with good culture (design/architecture culture here) . Often times poor "new" solutions are just the same mistake again
